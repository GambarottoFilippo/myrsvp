Personal event website with RSVP module

Show off your event with a free personal web page. Are you providing additional services to your guests like baby chairs or halal menu?
Great! Your invitees will be able to indicate if they need these services when they RSVP from your invitation page.

Invitation card editor

Unlimited co-hosts

Share the workload with your loved ones! When you add someone to your private invitees list you can also tag them as co-host. They can then add your event to MyRSVP app and they'll see it on the "co-hosted" page.

Co-hosts can help you with everything from their phones but they won't be able to modify the event details or purchase pro features on your behalf.

Private invitees list & public link

A private invitees list allows you to control who's in, and for each person you can choose how to send your invitation (for example whatsapp or mail).
Private invitations contain a unique link relative to that person RSVP, so should't be shared around

Or, you can share a public link, so anyone with the link can reply. In this case you always have control on when to deactivate the public link (for example if you already reached a limit number of guests).

Add attendees without sending an invitation

What if you already know that someone is definetly coming and you don't need to send out the invitation,
or if a person is not really tech savy (like most grandmas out there)?
Well, you can add attendees manually and RSVP on their behalf! Just go over the guest list page and tap on the + button :D

Arrange tables

Arranging seats for your event has never been easier! Sort out your guests with smart filtering, split groups and move guests across tables.
You can search for guests to see where they're seated and you'll also see who has arrived, who's missing and who's is extra while registration progresses!

Guest registration & last-minute changes

It's the big day! A guest brought extra people? Someone is missing?
No worries! you can register only individual people within a group, or quicly add extra guests and rearrange seats accordingly!

And you don't have to do it alone! The guest registration list is synced across yours and your co-host devices, so you will work on the same list, in real time ;)

Guests self-registration

In addition to traditional registration, guests can also self-register their presence by simply following the link on their RSVP confirmation email, or by using MyRSVP app.

This function will be active only on event day and you can check the registration confirmation on their phone.
That's all! Your registration list will update automatically ;)

Export guests list and table plan to PDF

Export your guest list and seating arrangements to detailed pdf files.
For the guest list, you have the option to organize it by guests relationship or simply by alphabetical order.

Angpao Registry

Your guests have been generous and you need to keep track of all those envelops? Divide them among your co-hosts so everyone can quickly record guests' angpows amounts on a shared list!

Recurrent events

What about all those small recurrent occasions like weekly futsal or monthly company dinner?
Just mark an event as recurrent and set its timing: once that event its over, MyRSVp will automatically update the time, keep your invitees list and reset the attendance list!

Smart filters

You will be dealing with long lists of names so it is important that you find who you're looking for quickly. Filter by alphabet, attendance status, RSVP preferences, and relationship.
Are you looking for attending vegetarian friends? Filter, done.
